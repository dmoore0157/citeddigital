Common Questions

Got questions?

If you don't see yours here, email us directly. We answer personally.

How is Cited Digital different from big consulting firms?
Big firms send partners to sell and junior associates to deliver. We're the opposite — our founders scope AND deliver every engagement. You also won't pay the overhead of someone else's office tower in Manhattan. Our pricing reflects what the work actually costs, not a corporate markup.
Do I need to have data infrastructure or AI knowledge first?
No. Most of our clients are starting from zero. That's exactly what Phase 01 (the AI Readiness Assessment) is for — we audit what you have, identify what's needed, and give you a realistic roadmap. If your data is a mess, we'll tell you that too.
What industries do you work with?
Our sweet spot is mid-market companies in regulated or operationally complex industries: healthcare, logistics and trucking, manufacturing, financial services, agriculture, and government/education. The common thread is that mistakes matter — and we're comfortable working in environments where compliance, documentation, and precision are non-negotiable.
How quickly can we start?
From first conversation to signed assessment is typically 2–4 weeks. Assessments run 2–4 weeks. Pilots run 4–12 weeks. You can go from initial call to working AI pilot in 3–4 months.
What does a typical engagement cost?
Assessments: $10K–$25K depending on scope. Pilot implementations: $25K–$75K. Ongoing fractional AI operations: $5K–$15K per month. We give fixed-fee pricing, not hourly estimates that balloon.
What if we decide not to move forward after the assessment?
That's fine. You keep the roadmap, the prioritized opportunities, and the projected ROI analysis. We only take on pilot engagements when there's a clear fit and a client who's ready to move. We'd rather say "not right now" than deliver a mediocre engagement.
Are you based in Kentucky only?
We're headquartered in Murray, Kentucky, and most engagements are within a day's drive — KY, TN, IN, MS, and OH. We'll travel farther for the right engagement. Much of our delivery is done remotely with on-site discovery and workshops as needed.
